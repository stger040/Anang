# Company name ideas — vet domains + Minnesota SOS + USPTO

**Chosen company:** **Anang** — [anang.ai](https://anang.ai). This file is **archived research**; keep for ideas when naming sub-products or future entities.

For any **new** name: **.com / .health / .ai**, **MN Secretary of State** business search, and **USPTO TESS** (see `docs/BRANDING.md` workflow).

**Names we previously flagged as crowded / risky** (still check yourself): Helix, Ember, Zephyr (health AI overlap), Iris (healthcare AI).

## Invented / coined (often easier to trademark)

Mix syllables you like, e.g. **Velon**, **Nexora**, **Lumena**, **Ardentis**, **Covieo**, **Truvera** — say aloud, check spelling on phone, then SOS/domain/USPTO.

---

## After you pick 2–3 finalists

1. **MN SOS** — [Business filings search](https://mblsportal.sos.state.mn.us/BusinessSearch) (official MN portal; confirm current URL).  
2. **Domain** — registrar + typosquat check.  
3. **USPTO** — Classes 9 & 42 for software/SaaS.  
4. **Google** — `"Name" healthcare` and `"Name" revenue cycle`.  
5. Update **`packages/brand/src/config.ts`** and optionally **`technical.serviceId`** for IT questionnaires.
